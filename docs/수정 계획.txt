### 2. Sentiment Analyst ➔ Replaced with Market Risk Analyst

In investing in ETFs or government bonds, the sentiment of individual investors on Naver stock discussion boards or Twitter is merely noise and holds no value.

### 3. Trader ➔ Replace with Portfolio Allocator

This is the most critical change. While the existing agent makes decisions to "Buy" or "Sell" specific stocks, in asset allocation competitions, you must determine "Portfolio Weights." The DB GAP competition evaluates MDD (Maximum Drawdown) and Sharpe Ratio (Risk-Adjusted Return) as importantly as absolute returns.

* **Original Logic:** Purchase 100 shares of Stock A based on Conviction.

* **Modified Logic:** Set the total assets to 100% and calculate weights (Weighting) as follows: 40% Stock ETF, 40% Bond ETF, 10% Commodities/Gold, 10% Cash, etc.

* **Prompt and Tool Modification:**

* If the LLM is left to make simple weight decisions, mathematical optimization fails.

* Therefore, you must implement this by integrating a library like Python's `PyPortfolioOpt` as a tool. The LLM determines only parameters such as "risk aversion level" or **"target return"**, while the actual weight calculations (Risk Parity, Black-Litterman models, etc.) are performed by external mathematical algorithms that then return the results to the LLM.

** ### 4. Technical Analyst and Target Ticker

---

### 1. Data Extraction & Parsing Skills

These skills automate the 'document reading' process, where the most errors occur. Instead of the LLM manually reading and summarizing the data, the data is structured through code.

* **`extract_financial_tables` (Precise extraction of financial statement tables):**
* **Function:** Ignores body text from SEC EDGAR 10-K (annual report), 10-Q (quarterly report), or PDF files, and accurately targets only **table data** such as 'Income Statement' and 'Balance Sheet' to extract them into CSV or Pandas DataFrame format.**
* **Significance:** Fundamentally prevents hallucinations caused by misrecognizing shifted numbers or merged cells.

* **`parse_earnings_transcript` (Structured parsing of earnings calls):**
* **Function:** Separates audio transcripts of earnings announcements spanning dozens of pages into 'Prepared Remarks' and 'Analyst Q&A'.

** * **Significance:** Filters only the CEO's defensive responses to sharp questions from specific analysts, or extracts only the guidance figures for the next quarter using regular expressions.

* **`compare_document_deltas` (Tracking changes between documents):**

* **Function:** Compares the 'Risk Factors' section of last year's business report with this year's to identify and highlight only phrases that the company has quietly added or deleted (e.g., the addition of "concerns about supply chain instability").

### 2. Quantitative Math & Modeling Skills

This skill involves completely delegating 'mathematical calculations,' the biggest weakness of LLM, to external systems.

* **`execute_python_sandbox` (Isolated Code Execution and Mathematical Operations):**

* **Function:** When LLM calculates WACC (Weighted Average Cost of Capital), DCF (Discounted Cash Flow), bond duration, etc., it does not output numbers directly; instead, it writes Python code, executes it in a sandbox environment, and retrieves only the resulting values.

* **`build_3_statement_model` (Building a 3-Statement Excel Model):**

* **Function:** Based on extracted historical financial data, it generates an Excel file (.xlsx) where the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement are linked by formulas.

* **Significance:** It performs the core task of an IB (Investment Banking) analyst: if commanded to "assume a sales growth rate of 5%," it enters values ​​into Excel input cells and generates a result sheet.

* **`run_comparable_analysis` (Companies Benchmarking / Comps):**

* **Function:** Identifies groups of competitors similar to the target company, calculates multiples such as market capitalization, EV/EBITDA, and P/E (Price-to-Earnings Ratio) for each company, and derives the average/median.

### 3. External Data and Terminal Integration Skills (Connectors & RAG)

This skill communicates with paid enterprise databases through MCP (Model Context Protocol), a core philosophy of Anthropic.

* **`query_market_data_api` (Retrieving Real-time/Historical Market Data):**

* **Function:** Calls APIs from data terminals subscribed to by actual financial firms, such as FactSet, S&P Capital IQ, Bloomberg, and Reuters, to retrieve stock prices, macroeconomic indicators (interest rates, unemployment rates, etc.), and consensus estimates.

** * **`semantic_internal_search` (Internal Document RAG Search):**

* **Function:** Rather than simple keyword search, it searches for historical cases most similar to the current market situation within the company's internally built historical research reports or Investment Committee (IC) memo databases using a vector-based approach and injects them into the context.

### 4. Output & Formatting Skill

This skill is designed for the automation of report writing and enforces 'standard compliance' over 'writing skills'.

* **`generate_pitchdeck_structure` (Generate Pitch Deck/PPT Structure):**

* **Function:** Converts text analysis results to fit PowerPoint slide formats. (e.g., Page 1 for summary, Page 2 for market analysis, Page 3 for financial charts, etc.). It exports slide titles, bullet points, and chart data separately.

** * **`enforce_json_schema` (Strict JSON Structuring):**

* **Function:** Controls that the agent's final output must be output exclusively in a predefined JSON format. This is an essential skill to prevent parsing errors when displaying data on frontend dashboards or transmitting data to the next agent (e.g., Portfolio Manager).

### 5. Risk & Compliance Skills

This skill resolves bottlenecks in back-office operations.

* **`check_kyc_aml_rules` (Anti-Money Laundering/Customer Due Diligence):**

* **Function:** Calculates a risk rating by comparing information on new clients or entities against the OFAC (Office of Foreign Assets Control) sanctions list or internal watchlist.

* **`validate_investment_mandate` (Verification of investment compliance):**

* **Function:** Pre-screens whether the portfolio weighting proposed by the fund manager (or agent) violates fund mandates, such as "prohibition on exceeding 10% of a single asset" or "prohibition on investing in bonds with a credit rating of BBB or lower."
